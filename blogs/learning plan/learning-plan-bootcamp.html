Bootcamp Learning Plan

My plan

What are your long term goals

I’m hoping to learn how to have a good grasp on the fundamentals of software development and understand how all of the parts of software development work together. I want to be in a position where I can hit the ground running (or at least walking) when I get a developer position. I think eventually it would be really cool to be able to be a tech nomad.

What do you think your biggest strengths and limitations will be in Bootcamp?

Resilience will be my greatest strength. I think my greatest weakness will be allocation of my time.

What do you think your biggest non-technical challenge at Bootcamp will be?

Probably just knowing when to ask for help. I tend to try figure out everything by myself. I’m working on it.

What non-technical skills - human skills - would you like to see developed in yourself while at Dev Academy?

Developing some leadership skills and Communication

What are your expectations from the Bootcamp team?

Get a basic understanding of a large chuck of software that developers use and how to begin using them effectively for yourself. I would also like to learn a little more computer literacy to be able to understand more of the complex and integral ways the operating systems work.

What are your expectations of yourself on Bootcamp

I’m hoping that once we start at Re-leased I will have to rely less on slack and online conversations to get help and will be able to talk to other team members and support face to face.
